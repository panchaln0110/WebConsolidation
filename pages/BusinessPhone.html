Call Forwarding

ACN offers a variety of call forwarding options such as:

  1. Automatically forward incoming calls to an alternate number when you’re on the go
  2. Forward callers to an alternate number when you are on the line
  3. Set your incoming calls to ring to multiple numbers simultaneously

Do Not Disturb

With Do Not Disturb, you can relax - all incoming calls will go straight to Voicemail. Your callers will simply hear a message saying you aren't accepting calls at this time. Plus, it won't affect your outgoing calls.

Anonymous Call Rejection

With Anonymous Call Rejection, your phone won't even ring if the caller blocks their Caller ID. They'll automatically hear a message saying you don't accept anonymous calls. If the person's Caller ID information simply isn't available, they won't be blocked.

Caller ID

With Caller ID, you can see who is calling, giving you the power to decide which calls you want to answer and which ones you want to send to voicemail. May not be available in all areas.

Call Return + Last Number Redial

With Call Return, you can automatically dial the number of your last incoming call, whether you answered it or not. Plus, dial the last number you called from your phone.

Caller ID Blocking

Don't want the person you're calling to know your number? Block it! You can block Caller ID when you pick up the phone to make the call.
